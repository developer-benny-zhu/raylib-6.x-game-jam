  <h3 align="center">The War Of Hex</h3>

  <p align="center">
    The War Of Hex is a turn-based strategy game taking place on a hexagonal grid out in who knows where. You will start off with 10 gold and you can use that to buy peasants (10), footmen (20) or tanks (50). You can spawn these troops by clicking on your base (in the bottom left). Move your troops to tiles to gain passive income. End your turn by pressing ENTER. Take the enemy base to merge their empire into yours.
